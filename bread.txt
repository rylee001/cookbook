CHAPTER 1. BREAD

Recipe 1.1: Basic bread 
Ingredients: 
- flour
- salt
- water 
- yeast
Instructions: 
-bloom yeast with 2 T lukewarm water
-mix ingredients
-knead for 20 minutes 
-leave to rise for at least one hour 
-knead until dough becomes stretchy
-let rise another 25 minutes 
-bake at 425 F for 25 minutes
